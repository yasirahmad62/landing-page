About

A fintech startup based in Delhi makes digital payment processing device-independent which means users don't have to carry a device to make payments with services like bill splitting, offline payments, and one to many payment users can seamlessly transact with each other. And when you don't have a device or in a bit hurry to scan QR Codes or get in queues to swipe up your physical card? SabPay makes it hassle-free for you, just touch and pay with your fingerprints and let us handle all the things for you. our proprietary algorithms powered by blockchain encrypt user information and transactions so that our users are always secured.

Features.

01

Pay & Receive

Now accept payments and pay to any user with blazingly fast speed.

02

Group Payments

Group Pay allows users to split bills effortlessly in a restaurant or to any local vendor when they are with their friends or colleagues.

03

Offline Payments

Off Pay is SabPay’s proprietary payment feature that allow two users to transact locally with SabPay wallet without internet and network connection.

04

One2Many Payments

One2Many feature lets you pay to users with different bill amounts all at once.

05

HelpDesk

Helpdesk, a 24X7 help center which allows users to raise a complaint or helps them to resolve any queries related to their payments.
